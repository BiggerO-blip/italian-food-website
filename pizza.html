Pizza

Naples, Italy

History

Modern pizza originated in Naples in the 18th century. It began as affordable food for working class people and later became popular across Italy and the world.

Best Traditional Version

The most famous version is Neapolitan pizza, made with simple dough, San Marzano tomatoes, frash mozzarella, basil, and olive oil.

Where to Eat It

Naples, Italy - especially traditional pizzaries that follow classic methods.

Classic Neapolitan Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • San Marzano tomatoes
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Fresh basil
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix flour, salt, yeats, and water to form dough.
  2. Knead and let rise for several hours.
  3. Stretch dough into a thin round.
  4. Add tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil.
  5. Bake at very high heat until crust bubbles.
